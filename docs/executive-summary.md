## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Will this replace my billing staff?**  
A: No. It makes them more efficient. Most practices reduce staff through attrition or reassign to higher-value work (denial management, patient calls). Small practices can go from 2 FTE to 1 FTE.

**Q: How accurate is the AI coding?**  
A: 94.2% for ICD-10, 91.8% for CPT codes. Your staff always reviews before submission. It's "assisted coding" not "autonomous coding."

**Q: What if it makes a mistake?**  
A: Your biller catches it during review. If a mistake gets through (rare), we have E&O insurance and audit protection. You're never liable for AI suggestions.

**Q: Do I need to change my EMR?**  
A: No. We integrate with most EMRs or work standalone. You keep your clinical workflow.

**Q: What about my current billing company?**  
A: You can keep them and use NexClinical as their tool (saves them time = saves you money). Or transition in-house with 1-2 staff + our system.

**Q: What's the catch?**  
A: Honest answer: Works best with practices doing >200 claims/month. Below that, cost/benefit is marginal. Also requires decent clinical documentation (garbage in = garbage out).
